Lemon Garlic Chicken Pasta

Prep 25 mins
Serves

Ingredients

  • 400 g spaghetti
  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
